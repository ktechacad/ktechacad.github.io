About Technology Awareness

The Technology Awareness Program by Cisco, SCO & TEVTA is a comprehensive three-month youth development initiative designed to empower participants with practical knowledge and industry-relevant skills in digital literacy, IT fundamentals, networking, cybersecurity, artificial intelligence, and freelancing. This program focuses on awareness, hands-on learning, ethical practices, and career readiness, enabling youth to confidently navigate the modern digital landscape and explore sustainable technology-driven career paths.

Course Objectives & Key Learning Outcomes

  • Develop digital awareness and understanding of modern digital technologies.
  • Gain practical knowledge of commonly used digital and business applications.
  • Understand IT essentials including computer hardware, operating systems, and troubleshooting.
  • Build strong foundations in networking concepts and network essentials.
  • Learn cybersecurity fundamentals including threats, vulnerabilities, and defense mechanisms.
  • Develop awareness of system, network, and endpoint security practices.
  • Understand the basics of Artificial Intelligence, Machine Learning, and data concepts.
  • Apply AI concepts in daily life and industry-relevant use cases.
  • Gain hands-on experience with Microsoft Office and productivity tools.
  • Learn safe computing practices, data privacy, online security, and workplace ethics.
  • Build strong foundations for online freelancing and remote digital work.
  • Learn how to offer digital services on international freelancing platforms.
  • Develop skills required for sustainable online income and self-employment.
